CAPACITY BUILDINGWhat We Do

We facilitate learning and strengthen the skills of local entrepreneurs and small- and medium-sized businesses to stimulate economic growth. We design and deliver a range of programmes including local capacity to help incubate start-up businesses and support companies with growth potential. We use the knowledge of our core team to work with local partners and involve expert associates.

Capacity building is more than training. It is the establishment of sustainable mechanisms that allow a community or nation to empower itself by developing its human capital. Developing local citizens better prepares a community or nation to face and resolve development issues in a responsible and sustainable manner.

Capacity building strategies and contributions depend on the specific context of each circumstance. Breinscope is committed to fostering capacity building initiatives that will empower each host community, and subsequently the host nation. Our long-term aim is to create a more favorable business environment.

We have has helped build capacity by successfully implementing our workforce integration philosophy in countries where we operate. We have established institutional capacity building programs that engage local government officials and industry leaders. These programs include workshops targeting development of specific professional skills and business practices.
