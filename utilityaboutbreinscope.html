ABOUT USWho We Are And What We Do

Breinscope Consultants Limited has offices in Nairobi and Kisumu. It is a Kenyan owned was first registered as a consultancy firm in the year 2002, and transformed into a limited liability company in July 2007. Its charge is to ensure that policy makers, senior managers, practitioners and local stakeholders have the knowledge, tools and capacity to make informed choices about performance and service delivery. Breinscope Consultants Limited has built a strong reputation as a leader in Capacity building of organizations programming, M&E and RBM that has contributed to enhancing the impact of development policies, programs and projects regionally.

Working extensively in the field of public and private sector development over the last 10 years, Breinscope Consultants Limited has undertaken numerous results-focused M&E assignments and provided technical assistance and training to a wide spectrum of public sector practitioners. Breinscope Consultants Limited has worked with bilateral and multilateral agencies, national governments, not-for-profit organizations, and the private sector from all continents.

Transforming complex needs with simple solutions

Our Offices in Nairobi Our Offices in Western Kenya
Breinscope Consultants Limited is a multi-disciplinary team of professionals including sociologists, economists and trade experts, education experts, scientists, health experts, public finance specialists, disaster/emergency management specialists, RBM and M&E senior consultants that provide a wide range of project management, training and capacity development services and a range of M&E services. Maintaining a privileged partnership with many bilateral and multilateral donor organizations and development banks for over a decade, we have successfully led numerous mandates that have helped us develop knowledge and capacity while increasing our number of partners and our presence around the development realm.

Breinscope Consultants Limited’s strength lies in its ability to combine consultative and participatory approaches with sound data collection and analytical techniques to identify and assess issues of critical relevance for optimizing performance and long-term capacity building.

Breinscope Consultants Limited works with clients at all stages of the project lifecycle, adding value through its in-depth understanding of cross-cultural relationships and its firm grasp of sustainable development concepts. These capabilities are further enriched by Breinscope Consultants Limited’s in-house capabilities and extensive network of associates, technical specialists and strategic partners. As a company dedicated to professionalism and quality, Breinscope Consultants Limited has made client satisfaction a priority.
